UTPHAX 2015

Total Award - RM15,000

Prizing Breakdown

    1. Bounty Award
    This award is given to the team that first solve a challenge. The breakdown are as follows:

    • Group Stage - RM 2500
      There are 5 rounds of match at the group stage. At each round there is a "jackpot" question where the first team to solve the challenge receives an award of RM500.
        • Round 1 - Yolo (APU)
        • Round 2 - N0ob13s (KPTM Alor Setar)
        • Round 3 - Alfarabi 2.0 (UiTM Melaka)
        • Round 4 - EZ (APU)
        • Round 5 - Alfarabi 2.0 (UiTM Melaka)

    • Semi-Final Stage - RM 1000
      There is a jackpot challenge in the semi-final stage where the first solver are rawarded with RM1000.
        • Semi-Final - Tak_tau (UTP)

    • Final Stage - RM 7800
      Unlike earlier stage, all 10 challenges at Final stage have a bounty award. Any teams that first solve the challenges will receive an award. However, each challenge will have a different value of award and are based on difficulty. Challenges that are not listed below have not been solved.
        • Challenge 3 (RM 1,000) - Grumpy Unicorn (MMU)
        • Challenge 5 (RM 1,000) - CHECKMATE (UNIKL)
        • Challenge 6 (RM 1,000) - Kairo (UiTM Shah Alam)
        • Challenge 8 (RM 1,500) - Yolo (APU)
        • Challenge 9 (RM 1,500) - Yolo (APU)
        • Challenge 10 (RM 1,800) - Grumpy Unicorn (MMU)

    2. Top Scorer Award - RM 3700
    This award is only entitled to the Finalist. This award are decided on the total challenges solved by each team, and does not take into account either they are the first or last solver. The breakdown is as follows:
    • Gold Scorer RM 1,500 - Grumpy Unicorn (MMU)
    • Silver Scorer RM 1,200 - Yolo (APU)
    • Bronze Scorer RM 1,000 - Kairo (UiTM Shah Alam)
